What is ABSA?

Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA) is a form of natural language processing (NLP) or natural language understanding (NLU).

It uses a technique to break down text, such as customer feedback and product reviews, into aspects (attributes or components) and then focuses on predicting the postive and negative polarity of the determined aspects.
